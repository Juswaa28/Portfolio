Projects

This capstone project focused on the formation and development of a new queuing transaction system, composed to have a faster, easier, and more efficient way for the Bago City College Registrar’s office. “Bago City College Registrar Office Queuing and Appointment Management System” is the title of this capstone project. The registrar’s office transaction and processes will be automated by the technology. They frequently conduct daily transaction and activities using manual methods. The issues and mistakes that arise during the process will be eliminated by this method. Paper data collected was used, along with in-personal interviews with the Registrar Office personnel at Bago City College.
